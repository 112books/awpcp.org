## Data controller

A.W.P.C.P. (Around the World's Pinhole Crazy People)  
Email: [info@awpcp.org](mailto:info@awpcp.org)

## Data we collect

We collect minimal personal data:

- **Contact form submissions**: name and email address, used only to respond to your inquiry.
- **Analytics**: anonymised page view statistics via GoatCounter. No cookies are used for analytics.

## How we use your data

Your data is used solely for the purpose for which it was provided — responding to your message or improving the website experience.

## Data retention

We retain contact form submissions for a maximum of 12 months.

## Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at [info@awpcp.org](mailto:info@awpcp.org) to exercise these rights.
