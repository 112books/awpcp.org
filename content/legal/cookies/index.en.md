## Cookie usage

This website uses **no tracking cookies**. We use **GoatCounter** for privacy-friendly analytics, which does not set cookies or track individual users.

## Essential cookies

No essential cookies are set by this website.

## Third-party cookies

Embedded content from platforms like YouTube or Vimeo (when present on edition pages) may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.

## Managing cookies

Since this site does not set any cookies, no cookie management is required on your part.
