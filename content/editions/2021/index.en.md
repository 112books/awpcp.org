The 3rd edition of the Worldwide Pinhole Photography Day in Barcelona, held virtually.

The 3rd edition of the Worldwide Pinhole Photography Day in Barcelona took place on the **roof of the Palomar** at **Nau Bostik**.

**Alfonso de Castro** and **Joan Linux** took pinhole portraits of participants using this ancestral technique. The activity was simple: just sit still for a few seconds while the light painted your portrait through a tiny hole.

> From 11am on the roof of the Palomar de la Nau Bostik.
