**COVID edition — the self-confined project.** With the lockdown preventing our usual gathering at Can Basté, we turned the 2020 edition into an online collaboration. Participants from around the world sent in videos presenting their favourite pinhole cameras. The result: a global virtual gallery of 19 cameras from 14 countries, connected by a live map.
