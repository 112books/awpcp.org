The 5th edition of the Worldwide Pinhole Photography Day was a relaxed gathering at **Sala Basiana** (inside **Nau Bostik**).

Not much was formally planned — just pinhole enthusiasts meeting up, sharing cameras, taking photos, and enjoying the day.

> Stop by the Sala Basiana with a pinhole camera (or similar andromeda) and celebrate the day of this primitive photographic technique with other interested people.
