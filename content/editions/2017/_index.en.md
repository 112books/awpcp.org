Worldwide Pinhole Photography Day 2017 at Nau Bostik, as part of the **LomoTaller** project in collaboration with Lomography.com, organized by Joan Linux from Lomography Embassy Barcelona.

### LomoTaller Project: 10 photographers, 10 perspectives

We invited ten photographers to experiment with a **Diana F+ in pinhole mode** during Pinhole Day. Some had never shot pinhole before; others had, but never with such a peculiar camera.

Although the interviews and photos were originally intended for Lomography's website, it didn't work out. Here are the results:
